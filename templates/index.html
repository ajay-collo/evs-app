Why It Matters

Our Environment Is In Crisis

Climate change, deforestation, and pollution are accelerating at an alarming rate. Education is the most powerful tool we have to reverse this trend.

🌡️
1.1°C
Global Temperature Rise
Earth's average temperature has risen by 1.1°C since pre-industrial times. Scientists warn we must limit this to 1.5°C to avoid catastrophic consequences for all life on Earth.
🌳
15B
Trees Lost Every Year
15 billion trees are cut down globally each year. Deforestation destroys habitats, reduces biodiversity, and accelerates climate change at an alarming pace worldwide.
🌊
8M
Tonnes of Plastic in Oceans
8 million tonnes of plastic enters our oceans annually, killing over 1 million seabirds and 100,000 marine mammals every single year through entanglement and ingestion.
💧
600M
Indians Face Water Stress
Over 600 million people in India face high-to-extreme water stress. By 2030, India's water demand is projected to be twice the available supply if action is not taken now.
🏭
36B
Tonnes of CO₂ Per Year
Humans emit 36 billion tonnes of CO₂ annually. This greenhouse gas is the primary driver of global warming, extreme weather, rising sea levels, and ecosystem collapse.
🦋
1M+
Species Threatened
Over 1 million animal and plant species face extinction. Biodiversity loss threatens the very ecosystems that provide food, clean water, clean air, and climate stability.
Activities We Cover

Key Environmental Activities

Students participate in real-world activities that make a direct impact on the environment. Click any activity to learn more.

🌱
Carbon Offset
Tree Plantation
Students plant trees in local areas, recording species, location, and photos as digital proof. Each tree planted contributes to carbon absorption and biodiversity restoration.
♻️
Zero Waste
Waste Management
Segregation of dry, wet, and hazardous waste. Students learn proper disposal methods and the importance of the circular economy in reducing landfill pressure.
💧
Water Security
Water Conservation
Rainwater harvesting, water audit campaigns, and leak detection drives. Students measure water usage and suggest conservation strategies for their institutions.
☀️
Clean Energy
Energy Awareness
Auditing energy consumption in classrooms and labs. Students identify wastage points and propose renewable energy solutions for their campuses.
🌿
Soil Health
Composting
Converting organic waste into compost. Students manage compost bins, track decomposition rates, and use outputs for campus gardens and green spaces.
🐦
Ecosystem
Biodiversity Survey
Identifying and documenting local flora and fauna. Students conduct ecological surveys and understand the importance of preserving native species.
Simple Process

How It Works

From QR code to verified participation — the entire process is digital, fast, and transparent.

1
Teacher Creates Activity
Teacher fills in activity details and the system generates a unique QR code instantly.
2
QR Code Displayed
QR code is printed or projected at the activity location on campus.
3
Student Scans QR
Student scans using any phone browser. Task instructions appear immediately.
4
Submit Proof
Student uploads photo or typed answer as evidence of completing the task.
5
Teacher Verifies
Teacher reviews submissions and approves or rejects with detailed feedback.
Platform Features

Everything You Need

Built for schools and colleges — powerful for teachers, simple for students.

📱
QR Code Activities
Teachers generate unique QR codes for each environmental activity. Students scan and get task instructions instantly on any smartphone — no app download needed.
📸
Photo Proof Submission
Students upload photographic evidence of completed activities. Tamper-resistant, time-stamped digital records replace manual registers permanently.
⏱️
Auto Attendance Tracking
Attendance is recorded automatically when a student scans a QR code. Duration is tracked without any manual intervention from teachers or staff.
📊
Analytics Dashboard
Teachers see class-wide performance charts. Students track their own progress. Data-driven insights for better learning outcomes and institutional reports.
✅
Teacher Verification
All student submissions go through teacher review. Approve, reject, or edit records with feedback notes for continuous improvement and accountability.
🔐
Secure Role-Based Access
Three separate portals for Students, Teachers, and Admins. Each role sees only what they need — secure, organized, and efficient for every user.
